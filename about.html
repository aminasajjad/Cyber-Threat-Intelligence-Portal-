About Portal

Project Purpose

This Cyber Threat Intelligence Portal is developed by a first semester Cyber Security student to spread awareness about cyber attacks. The purpose of a Cyber Threat Intelligenc Portal is to gather , analyze and share cyber threat information to enhance security , prevent attacks and support effective incident response.

Why Cyber Awareness Matters

In today's digital world, everyone uses the internet. Lack of awareness can lead to hacking, fraud and data theft. Cyber awareness protect personal information and online identity.
