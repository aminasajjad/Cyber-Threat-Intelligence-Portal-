Cyber Safety Tips

Strong Passwords

Use long and unique passwords.Avoid using easy or common information like your name and date of birth.Always use a different password for each important account. It should be include a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers and special characters.

Two Factor Authentication

Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account. It requires a second step of varification, such as a code sent to your phone or email. Even if someone knows your password, they cannot acces your account without this second step enabling 2FA greatly reduces the risk of account hacking.

Regular software update

Always keep your apps , operatinfg system, and antivirus software updated. Updates fix security weaknesses and protect your device from new threats.

Backup your data

Keep a backup of important files on cloud storage or an external drive. Backups help you recover data in case of device failure or cyber attacks.
